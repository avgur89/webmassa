About us

A complex advertizing campaigns is always a challenge, that leads to heavy expenses for overstaffed marketing departments. Some of them are non-professional, especially in web-promotion. So first You must pay a work of an advertizing department for the market research and an idea - and than find a good web-agency to bring the project to live. And it costs more time and money.

Webmassa Laboratory is a one-stop promotional solution for online and offline businesses. From the brand concept and corporate identity to the complex internet projects, product design and POS materials. With our experience in advertizing, webdesign and programming we can offer an all-round support for your brand.

How we do it?

First impressions are most lasting. Your trademark must be eye-catching. We know it fully well and can do it from a perfectly styled logo to a full brand identity. Our professional designers can draw it from scratch or refresh an existing visual identity and make it more remarkable.

Your project must go online to make it popular within a wide audience. We create full-responsive interfaces so our websites are fast and easily readable at any device. Our front-end and back-end developers always make it user-friendly and easy to administrate.

Interesting for a consumer. That is what your products should look like. We know to make every trade dress outstanding in every shop. Our packing design skills already made popular a number of product lines. And we're always ready to brand new thing.
