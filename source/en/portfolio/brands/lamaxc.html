LamaXC is a small bike park on the river Lama in Moscow region. Before we started to invite the first visitors using landing page, we had to create a good brand for this place.

Since the park is located on the hills near the river, we decided to show this in a shape of a logo. The word "Lama" was written in a way that reminds us both the contour of the hills and water stream.

This style continues in the shapes of Lama XC landing page blocks. This is what we call sport minimalism. Everything is simple and clear - that's how to deliver the main idea of a brand faster.
