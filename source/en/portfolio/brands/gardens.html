Gardens.pro is a brand of Elena Konstantinova - one of the most famous and successful landscape designers in Russia. For the new website of her team we made this logo.

The desired color was red, but the whole design should be soft to match the website style. So we decided to make the letters E&K as curved as possible, so this logo nice fits for biodesign. You can check out how does it look on www.gardens.pro
