DevTool is a webdesign and development blog, as You could see in a previous work describtion. And of course this project must have it's own own brand and logo. Web-development is not a joke, but we wanted to make this logo more playful, than other similar projects.

So we took one of the "tools for fun" - a corcscrew that was placed in curly brackets, like a javascript value. At the same time this corkscrew looks like letter "T" - the initial of "tool".

What about the colors? They're also playful - orange and green harmonize well giving our visitors good humor. DevTool brand makes the world of web-design more friendly!
