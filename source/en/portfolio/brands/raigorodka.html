Raigorodka logo

Raigorodka is a name of a place where this nursery farm was established. And it means "paradisal place". That's what we should capture in a company logo. We made it in a simple but perfect way.

First letter of the nursery name was stylized in a form of a tree stem. And then we add a green crown and the sun because it wouldn't be a paradise logo without it. This logo shows us well both the profile of the company and the main idea of this brand.
