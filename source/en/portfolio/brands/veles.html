Veles logo

Veles is a brand of a plant nursery farm in Russia. And it's not necessary to use green color if a company is specialized in plant growing. A lot of plant nurseries use trees contours and shades of green for their logos. But this brand is outstanding.

We took the first letter of a company name and put it in a burgundy coloured circle. Since the company name takes origin from the ancient russian idol Veles, we used old slavic fonts in a logo. Than we just add some outlines of leaves and flowers in a circle. And now the composition is complete and perfect.
