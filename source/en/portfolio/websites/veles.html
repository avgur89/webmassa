Veles is a website for plant nursery farm in Russia. It has modern minimalistic user interface and responsive layout. The clients of our nursery are landscape designers and gardeners - so this website was created in accordance with their requirements.

First of all, it's responsive. Since our visitors are business professionals they must have a good mobile experience. This website is also fast - we did our best in a performance optimization. And it has modern and clean eye-catching interface. This website is a significant part of a brand-management we realized for this nursery.
