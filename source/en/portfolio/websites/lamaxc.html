LamaXC is a cross-country MTB and ski park in Moscow region on the banks of river Lama. This website is an invitation for riders to train and improve their skills in this place.

So we decided to give them a hint about our purpose in a design of this landing-page. First, our logo has a contour that reminds us both the shape of the hills and water stream. Than we continued this conception with a smooth shapes of our information blocks and images.

As You see, everything is clear (in Russian, of course) and simple, with a responsive design. Now our website is tested, and our XC-park is ready to recieve it's visitors!
